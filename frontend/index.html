💡 Today's Safety Tips

🔴
No real bank or government agency will ever ask for your OTP, PIN, or password over a call or message.
🟡
Scammers create urgency on purpose — "act now or lose your account." Real services give you time.
🟢
When in doubt, hang up and call the official number from the back of your card or the company's website.
